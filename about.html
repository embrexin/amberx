VIVINOS

Vivinos is a South Korean artist, animator, and YouTuber. She is the creator and co-director of Alien Stage as well as many other projects, including the animated series Pink Bitch Club. Vivinos started off making fanmade animated music videos for existing songs such as Villain by Stella Jang. Her music video for Suki Suki Daisuki kickstarted her Youtube animation career in 2021. Most recently, Vivinos directed the animated music video for 偽顔 by Yama along with Alien Stage co-director Qmeng.

QMENG

Qmeng is a South Korean animator and Youtuber who co-directs the Alien Stage series with Vivinos. Other than Alien Stage, Qmeng has has created several other animation projects, including music videos for 僕は人狼 by ROI and ランニングアウト by YAMA. Qmeng started her career as a character design freelancer in 2016. Her most recent work other than the Alien Stage series was directing the animated music video for 偽顔 by Yama along with Alien Stage co-director Vivinos.
